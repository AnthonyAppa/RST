Info:

Make sure to do each of these exercises from a tough 5-12 repetitions for around 3-4 sets. You want to make sure you have good form and are pushing your self to failure. The more intense your workouts are the more tension is being placed on your muscle fibers allowing them to tear more. With more muscle fibers tearing, the more muscle growth you will achieve when you have recoverd. Overall, allowing you to achieve strength and muscle growth more rapidly.
