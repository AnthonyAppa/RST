Beginner



Range: 0 - 1 years



This course is created especially for beginners, and will walk you through your pathwway to build an amazing physique. This workout program will consists of 5 days in the gym, and the days will be aranged by bodypart. I designed this courses in order for beginners to build a good mind muscle connection, and will help people grow the fastest during their fist couple years working out.



Intermediate



Range: 1 - 3 years



This course was created for people are ready to take it up a notch from the beginner stage. This course will consist of 4 days in the gym but will now combine different body parts in the same day. I have created this course as now with some knowlege of lifting in the gym, the focus has shifted from only one body part to train more body parts in one week to optamize muscle growth.



Advanced



Range: 3+ years



This course was made for the people that have been working out for a long time and consider themselves advanced. This program was built to optamize your time in the gym and to focus on targeting as many muscle groups as possible in one week. This course will consist of 3 days in the gym however, it is recommeneded that you do this course twice a week in order to maximze muscle growth.
